Colonel Raj Kalra is a distinguished entrepreneur, ex-Army veteran, and visionary leader behind the Kuber Group Africa, where he serves as the proud Founder and Group Chairman. Headquartered in Accra, Ghana, the Kuber Group is a prominent eco-friendly infrastructure development partner dedicated to sustainable growth across Africa.

A testament to the company’s commitment to large-scale sustainable development is the successful creation of a 271-acre industrial park in Prampram. With a strong emphasis on Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) compliance, Colonel Kalra is at the forefront of the green energy transition.

Beyond his role at Kuber Group, Colonel Kalra is the Founder and Chairman of the Board of Directors of the International Process Excellence Council (IPEC). His dedication to quality is underscored by his certification in Total Quality Management (TQM) by the Japanese Union of Scientists and Engineers (JUSE) in Tokyo.

Brajesh Kumar Singh is a seasoned transformation leader and the Co-Founder and Global CEO of the International Process Excellence Council (IPEC). With over 27 years of experience, he has a proven track record of elevating performance and sustainability for enterprises across four continents, guiding them through large-scale digital and operational turnarounds.

In his prior roles as both Chief Operating Officer and Chief Sustainability Officer, Brajesh pioneered the adoption of AI-enabled analytics and robotic process automation to drive multi-million-dollar efficiencies.

A Six Sigma Master Black Belt and recipient of MIT’s prestigious Fire Hydrant Award, his leadership style blends disciplined Process Excellence with an entrepreneurial mindset.

Uttam Biswas is the Chief Executive Officer of IPEC and Director of Zinnia Digital Services LLP. A seasoned quality professional, he brings over 22 years of experience in business process re-engineering, training, and management consulting. His expertise spans diverse industries, including Digital & Platform, Manufacturing, Banking, Financial Services, Insurance, and Civil Infrastructure.

A Six Sigma Master Black Belt and Lean Coach, Uttam leverages methodologies such as Lean, Six Sigma, and Kaizen to drive significant cost reductions and revenue enhancements for organizations.

Sangeet Malhotra is the Chief Operating Officer of IPEC. He is a seasoned senior management professional with over 15 years of extensive experience in project planning, control, and digital transformation. A Prince2 Certified and PMP-trained project management expert, he specializes in ensuring projects succeed from inception to completion.

Sangeet’s core strengths lie in end-to-end project implementation and strategic planning. His expertise includes managing multiple projects on a global scale, standardizing processes across countries like the UK, US, France, and India.

Cdr. M. Ramanathan Kumar (Retd.) is the Head of International Certifications at IPEC. An experienced consultant and auditor, he specializes in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), Cybersecurity, Business Resilience, and Data Privacy. His expertise is built on a distinguished career spanning more than three decades, including 21 years of service in the Indian Navy as a submariner.

Commander Lingesh, an Indian Navy veteran, has nearly four decades of professional experience. His background spans two decades of distinguished military service as a Submariner, specializing in Telecommunications and Electronic Warfare, and two decades of expertise in Cybersecurity.

Commander Lingesh spent 15 years with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), where he served as the Cybersecurity Delivery Head.

Deepak Dwivedi is a transformative leader in process excellence and educational innovation, with over 27 years of experience. Founder of 4 Alpha AED Labs, he created the Four Alpha Model. A Six Sigma Black Belt from the Indian Statistical Institute and a Certified Knowledge Manager.

Mitanshu Bhasin is the Developer & Tech Associate at IPEC and the architect behind the IPEC Expense Manager platform. A dynamic member of the technical team, he specializes in Python development, full-stack web engineering, and cybersecurity protocols.

Mitanshu bridges the gap between theoretical computer science and real-world deployment. He is responsible for developing automated Python scripts for data handling, maintaining cyber hygiene standards across internal communications, and researching emerging STEM technologies for team integration.

Currently preparing for advanced engineering entrance examinations (JEE), he applies rigorous analytical training in Physics and Mathematics directly to algorithm optimization and software architecture. His work ethic aligns with professional standards far beyond his years.
